Common questions

Loan FAQs

Can I get a loan for agricultural land?
Most banks don't fund agricultural land directly. However, if the land has NA (Non-Agricultural) conversion or is in a RERA-approved layout, several NBFCs will fund it. We guide you through this.
What is the minimum salary for a ₹40L home loan?
Typically ₹35,000–40,000/month take-home for a 20-year loan. But this varies by bank and your existing EMIs. We assess your exact eligibility for free.
How long does loan approval take?
With complete documents, most banks approve in 7–15 working days. We help you prepare documents correctly so there are no delays.
Do you charge any fee?
Absolutely zero fee from you. We earn a referral commission from banks when your loan is disbursed. You get our full service free.
What documents are needed?
Basic documents: Aadhaar, PAN, 3 months salary slips (or 2 years ITR for self-employed), bank statements, and property documents. We give you a complete checklist.
Can I transfer my existing home loan?
Yes. If your current rate is above 9%, a balance transfer could save you ₹2L–8L over the remaining tenure. We calculate the exact benefit before you decide.
